Is Your AAC App Slow or Glitching?

Check your device End of Support Date - Updated April 2025

This guide helps you check if your device is still receiving software updates that are needed for AAC (Augmentative and Alternative Communication) apps to work properly. Devices that no longer receive updates often have performance issues.

Important Information

Devices that have reached their end of support date may still function, but they're more likely to experience:

  • Slow app performance
  • Unexpected crashes or glitches
  • Incompatibility with newer app versions
  • Security vulnerabilities

Recommended Minimum Specs for AAC Apps

  • Processor: 2GHz or faster
  • Memory (RAM): 2GB minimum, 4GB recommended
  • Storage: 16GB minimum, 32GB+ recommended
  • Operating System: Device with active support status

Note: As a rule of thumb, device manufacturers typically stop providing updates after 5-7 years from release date. AAC apps may continue working on older devices but with reduced functionality or performance issues.
